Family workshop with personal service

Tradition, workshop and showroom

Generational know-how, in-house glaze recipes and a showroom in Prague 6 create a direct buying experience instead of anonymous retail.

Family craft

The know-how goes back to the late 18th century, with a lasting focus on hand work, custom glaze recipes and useful decorative ceramics.

Apprentices and continuity

Since 1993 the workshop has cooperated with a vocational school and helped new generations learn the trade.

Direct customer service

The Prague 6 showroom allows visitors to see the assortment, compare glazes and discuss custom production directly with the makers.

Questions?

Frequently asked questions

How can I order KERAT pottery?

You can order through our online shop on Fler.cz or contact us directly at stepanek@kerat.cz. Larger and custom orders are handled individually.

Is custom production with a logo or motif possible?

Yes, we offer custom production including company logos, promotional ceramics and individual motifs. Contact us for pricing and details.

Which glazes are available from stock?

We keep 12 of the most popular designs in stock: glazes no. 1, 2, 3, 4, 14, 17, 30, 35, 36, 37, 38 and 39. The remaining 28 glazes are made to order.

Can I visit the showroom in person?

The showroom and workshop in Prague 6 Dejvice are open Monday to Friday, 08:00–17:00. Evening and weekend visits can be arranged in advance by phone or email.
