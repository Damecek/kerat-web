Story

Tradition, workshop and showroom

The Štěpánek family has passed pottery craft from generation to generation. A dedicated workshop opened in Dejvice in 1991 and has continued expanding its production and showroom ever since.

Family craft

The know-how goes back to the late 18th century, with a lasting focus on hand work, custom glaze recipes and useful decorative ceramics.

Apprentices and continuity

Since 1993 the workshop has cooperated with a vocational school and helped new generations learn the trade.

Direct customer service

The Prague 6 showroom allows visitors to see the assortment, compare glazes and discuss custom production directly with the makers.

The showroom and workshop are open Monday to Friday, 08:00–17:00, with evenings and weekends by arrangement. Come and see the collection and choose glazes in person.
